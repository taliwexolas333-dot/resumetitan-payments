Frequently Asked Questions

How fast will I get my documents? +
Most orders are delivered within 24 hours. Premium and Basic bundles may take up to 48 hours depending on complexity. We always prioritize speed without sacrificing quality.
How do I pay with cryptocurrency? +
Select Bitcoin (BTC), Polygon (POL), or Solana (SOL) during checkout. You'll get a unique invoice with the exact amount and our wallet address. Send the payment directly from your wallet — funds go straight to us, no intermediaries.
What is ATS optimization and why does it matter? +
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) software scans resumes before humans see them. We analyze the job description, extract key keywords, and weave them naturally into your resume. Most applicants get filtered out by ATS — we make sure you don't.
Can I get revisions if something isn't right? +
Absolutely. Every order includes one round of revisions. We want you to be thrilled with your documents. Just reply to your delivery email with feedback and we'll make it right.
What information do you need from me? +
Your current resume (or bullet points about your experience), the job description of the role you're targeting, and a few notes about your career goals. The more context you give us, the more tailored your documents will be.
